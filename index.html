Boyu Fan

About me

I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at MIT, where I work with Professor Triantaphyllos R. Akylas. I am funded as a NSF Graduate Research Fellow.

Prior to MIT, I was an undergrad at Caltech, where I worked with Mory Gharib and Julia R. Greer.

Research interests

I'm interested in a range of problems relating to the modeling of fluid systems, especially ones of the nonlinear flavor. Currently, I've been exploring various instabilities of internal gravity wave beams.
